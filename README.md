What would you put in their enclosure?

This project has different motivators and goals. Solve all problems beneficence, non-malificense, autonomy and justice. Erradicate violence, poverty, and injustice (whereby justice is defined as prevention only). To paraphrase Martin Luther King Jr "I have a ddream that one day every like black boy or girl will say to every little white boy or girl 'My grandpa told me in the olden days people used to hurt each other on purpose' and the other child will respond 'No way! That is so weird'". This dream is something acheivable. The zookeeper metaphore is poignant precicely because we are facing the same predicament as zookeepers were at one stage in history.
